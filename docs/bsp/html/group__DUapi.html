Initialise the given display unit.

DU driver does not have the DeInitialize API, therefore after called this function, target DU Unit can not close.

Parameters

[in]TimingsSee r_du_DispTimings_t (must not be NULL) Timings->CurrentDispClock may be modified for calculated PLL settings.
[in]DuPlaneMsklist of planes to be used. 1: plane1, 2: plane3, 3: plane 1&3
